PREMIUM CURRY SET

Premium Curry Set comes with rice, tender chicken, and a flavorful vegetable curry. A perfect combination of rich Sri Lankan spices and hearty comfort — ideal for a satisfying meal.

KOTTU

Kottu is a popular Sri Lankan street food made by stir-frying chopped flatbread (roti) with vegetables, eggs, meat, and a blend of flavorful spices. It's a sizzling, savory dish known for its rhythmic chopping sound and bold, satisfying taste — a true taste of Sri Lanka!

NOODLES

Sri Lankan Noodles are stir-fried egg noodles cooked with a mix of colorful vegetables, spices, soy sauce, and your choice of meat or seafood. Infused with Sri Lankan flavors and a hint of chili, it's a deliciously spicy and comforting dish enjoyed any time of day.

BIRYANI

Sri Lankan Biryani is a fragrant rice dish cooked with basmati rice, tender meat (chicken, mutton, or seafood), and aromatic spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves. Served with boiled egg, fried onions, and raita, it's a flavorful and hearty meal perfect for any special occasion.

FRIED RICE

Fried Rice is a flavorful stir-fried rice dish made with basmati rice, vegetables, eggs, and your choice of meat or seafood. Cooked with soy sauce and Sri Lankan spices, it's a delicious fusion of Asian taste with a local twist.
